In today's digital age, English proficiency is essential for accessing information and participating in online communities. The vast majority of content on the internet is in English, including websites, social media platforms, and online forums. Therefore, individuals with strong English skills have a distinct advantage in navigating the digital landscape and engaging with a global audience.

Moreover, English is the language of academia and research, with a significant portion of scholarly articles, journals, and conferences conducted in English. For students and professionals seeking to pursue higher education or advance their careers, the ability to read, write, and speak English fluently opens doors to a world of opportunities.
