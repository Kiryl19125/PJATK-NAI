English is an international language spoken by millions of people worldwide. It serves as a common means of communication in diverse fields such as business, science, technology, and entertainment. As a result, proficiency in English has become increasingly important for individuals seeking to succeed in the globalized economy.

The English language has a rich literary tradition, with iconic works ranging from the plays of William Shakespeare to the novels of Jane Austen and Charles Dickens. These literary masterpieces have not only entertained readers for centuries but have also contributed to shaping the English language itself, influencing vocabulary, grammar, and style.
